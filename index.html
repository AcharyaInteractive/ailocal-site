Vision

AILocal explores a local-first approach to AI development—one that keeps creators in control of their tools, data, and intellectual property rather than tethering creation to remote platforms.

Origin

This project emerged from a practical gap: the absence of tools that allow non-programmers to build real, modular software with AI while retaining autonomy. AILocal is a constructive response to that absence.
